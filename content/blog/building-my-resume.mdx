I had no idea what I was doing when I first put my resume together. A few months into first year, I had a CGPA, two projects I'd started working on, an internship, and a co-founded startup — but turning that into something readable was kind of tricky.

The first version was just a list with no context. "Worked on a startup." Great, doing what? Once I forced myself to answer *what I did* and *what came out of it*, the bullets got a lot sharper.
