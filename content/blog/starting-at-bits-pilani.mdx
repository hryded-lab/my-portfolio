Starting my second semester at BITS Pilani felt quite surreal. The first semester seemed to end in a flash and to be honest it was quite fun.

Coming to BITS I expected the environment to be extremely studious (which it is to a certain extent) but I was wrong. The environment on campus was full of life with so much going on socially that the studies didn't feel that hard.

Sure everyone is competitive but in a way, this helped me realize that there are many ways different people excel in different ways and interacting with these people had me learning like crazy.

So yeah, overall really looking forward to how the second semester plays out.
